The Solution

Why students struggle to translate coursework into workforce readiness, and where the current process breaks.

Solution Statement

GradPace is a university-integrated, Artificial-Intelligence-enabled career intelligence platform that connects academic progress with real-time professional opportunities. The system unifies academic records, local events, internships, certifications, and alumni engagement into a data-informed framework that continuously evaluates skill alignment and dynamically generates relevant next steps. By making readiness measurable and proactively matching students to opportunities, GradPace transforms reactive career navigation into structured, continuous development.

Solution Characteristics

  • Integrated Academic & Opportunity Intelligence

    GradPace brings academic records, experiential activities, local events, internships, certifications, and alumni resources into a single, continuously updated system. Instead of navigating disconnected platforms, students receive a structured and comprehensive view of their academic and professional development in one place.

  • Proactive, Dynamic Opportunity Matching

    GradPace continuously evaluates student progress and dynamically surfaces relevant opportunities based on skill development, academic standing, and career goals. Rather than manually monitoring listings, students receive prioritized, time-sensitive recommendations aligned with their trajectory.

  • Structured Skill Benchmarking & Readiness Transparency

    GradPace defines career-specific readiness benchmarks and compares student progress against local employer expectations and industry standards. By making skill gaps visible and measurable early, students can evaluate their preparedness with clarity rather than assumption.
