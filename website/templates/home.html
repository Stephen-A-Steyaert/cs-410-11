Choose your Path - Build your Future

The Problem

Universities clearly define academic degree requirements, yet professional development opportunities — including internships, certifications, networking events, alumni engagement, and local experiences — exist across separate platforms and resources. Students must independently connect their coursework to evolving market expectations without a unified, data-informed system to identify skill gaps or find relevant opportunities in real time. This structural disconnect leads to reactive decision-making and delayed workforce readiness.

Learn More

Our Solution

GradPace is a university-integrated, Artificial-Intelligence-enabled career intelligence platform that connects academic progress with real-time professional opportunities. The system unifies academic records, local events, internships, certifications, and alumni engagement into a data-informed framework that continuously evaluates skill alignment and dynamically generates relevant next steps. By making readiness measurable and proactively matching students to opportunities, GradPace transforms reactive career navigation into structured, continuous development.
